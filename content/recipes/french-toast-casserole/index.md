## Ingredients {#ingredients}


### Casserole {#casserole}

-   1 loaf challah or brioche bread
-   8 eggs
-   1-1/2 cups whole milk
-   1 cup heavy cream
-   1/2 cup brown sugar
-   1 tbsp vanilla
-   1 tsp cinnamon
-   1/2 tsp nutmeg


### Topping {#topping}

-   1/2 cup brown sugar
-   1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
-   2 tbsp flour
-   1 tsp cinnamon


## Directions {#directions}

{{< alert icon="circle-info" >}}
This casserole can be made up to 2 days in advance and kept in the refrigerator.
{{< /alert >}}

1.  Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
2.  Cube bread into 1 inch pieces, then spread in the bottom of a 13x9 baking
    dish.
3.  Whisk together remaining casserole ingredients and pour over bread, making
    sure to everything soaked.
4.  In a separate bowl, mix together the topping ingredients.
5.  Spread or layer over the casserole.
6.  Bake for 50 - 55 minutes until set and the top is golden.
