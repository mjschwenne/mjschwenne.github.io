## Ingredients {#ingredients}

-   2 cups fresh blueberries
-   1 cup sugar
-   1 cup water


## Directions {#directions}

1.  Add blueberries, water and sugar to a pot and cook over medium heat for about
    20 minutes, until it is thick like jam.
