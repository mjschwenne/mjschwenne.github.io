## Ingredients {#ingredients}

-   1 cup basmati rice
-   1 cup Toor Dal or Moong Dal
-   4 cups water
-   3 tsp salt
-   1/4 tsp turmeric
-   Achaar
-   Ghee


## Directions {#directions}

1.  Wash rice and dal thoroughly, the place in the Instant Pot.
2.  Add water, turmeric and salt.
3.  Pressure cook on high for 5 minutes, followed by a 10 minute natural release.
4.  Serve with achaar and ghee.
