## Ingredients {#ingredients}

-   2/3 to 1 cup quinoa
-   1 to 1-1/2 cup water or broth


## Directions {#directions}

1.  Rinse the quinoa under cold running water to remove the bitter coating.
2.  Add quinoa and liquid to Instant Pot. You need a 1:1-1/2 ratio here.
3.  Pressure cook for 2 minutes on high, then natural release for 10 minutes.
4.  Fluff with a fork before serving.
