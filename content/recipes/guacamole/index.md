## Ingredients {#ingredients}

-   2 avocados
-   hot sauce
-   minced garlic
-   scallions
-   seasoned salt
-   tomatoes


## Directions {#directions}

1.  Mash the avocados in a bowl.
2.  Add minced garlic, seasoned salt and scallions. Can also add tomatoes or hot
    sauce if desired.
