## Ingredients {#ingredients}

-   1 lbs ground beef
-   1 can corn
-   1 cup cheese
-   1 tsp garlic powder
-   1-1/2 lbs potatoes
-   1 can cream of mushroom soup


## Directions {#directions}

1.  Dice potatoes and place in slowcooker.
2.  Place all other ingredients in slowcooker and cook on low for 6 hours.
