## Ingredients {#ingredients}

-   1 small orange
-   3 cups raw cranberries
-   1-1/4 cup sugar


## Directions {#directions}

1.  Grind orange and cranberries.
2.  Mix in sugar and let set overnight in refrigerator before serving.
