## Ingredients {#ingredients}

-   2 lbs chicken
-   1 jar queso
-   1 jar salsa
-   1 tsp garlic powder


## Directions {#directions}

1.  Cook chicken in slowcooker on low for 6 hours.
2.  Drain juices.
3.  Add salsa, queso and garlic powder, then cook for an additional 30 minutes.
