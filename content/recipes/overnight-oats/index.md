## Ingredients {#ingredients}

-   1/2 cup old fashioned oats
-   1/2 milk
-   1/8 tsp cinnamon
-   1-1/2 tsp chia seeds
-   2 tsp brown sugar


## Directions {#directions}

1.  Combine all dry ingredients, then add milk.
2.  Mix well, refrigerate overnight.

{{< alert icon="circle-info" >}}
In the morning, I like to add a spoonful of honey or maple syrup to make it
slightly sweeter.
{{< /alert >}}
