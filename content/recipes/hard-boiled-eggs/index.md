## Ingredients {#ingredients}

-   1 to 12 eggs
-   1 cup water


## Directions {#directions}

1.  Place the egg steamer or trivet in the bottom of the instant pot.
2.  Add eggs without stacking them on top of each other.
3.  Add water.
4.  Pressure cook 5 minutes on high.
5.  Natural release for 5 minutes, then quick release any remaining pressure.
6.  Transfer eggs to an ice bath and soak for 5 minutes.
7.  Peel and serve.
