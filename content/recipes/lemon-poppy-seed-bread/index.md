## Ingredients {#ingredients}

-   18-1/4 oz package lemon cake mix
-   4 eggs
-   1/2 cup vegetable oil
-   3 oz instant lemon pudding mix
-   1 cup water
-   1/4 cup poppy seeds


## Directions {#directions}

1.  Preheat oven to 350 F. Grease and flour two 9x4 inch loaf pans.
2.  In a large bowl, mix the cake mix, eggs, oil, pudding mix, water and poppy
    seeds. Spread batter into greased loaf pans.
3.  Bake for 25 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
4.  Allow bread to cool for 10 minutes in pan before turning onto a wire rack to
    cool completely.
