## Ingredients {#ingredients}

-   Up to 4 frozen boneless skinless chicken breasts
-   1 cup water, chicken broth, apple juice, etc...
-   salt
-   pepper
-   other seasoning


## Directions {#directions}

1.  Place chicken in the bottom of the Instant Pot. It's OK if they are stacked,
    but they shouldn't be stuck together.
2.  Pour in chosen liquid and add desired seasoning.
3.  Pressure cook on high for 11 minutes, then natural release for 5 - 10 minutes
    before quick releasing any remaining pressure.
