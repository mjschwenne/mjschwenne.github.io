## Ingredients {#ingredients}

-   2 lbs chicken
-   2 cups cooked rice
-   peas
-   carrots
-   1 cup of cheese
-   1 can cream of chicken soup


## Directions {#directions}

1.  Preheat oven to 350 F.
2.  Add all ingredients to baking dish.
3.  Bake for 40 - 60 minutes.
