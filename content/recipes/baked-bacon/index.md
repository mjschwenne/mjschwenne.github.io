## Ingredients {#ingredients}

-   12 oz sliced bacon
-   brown sugar
-   paprika


## Directions {#directions}

1.  Lay the bacon in a single layer on a foil-lined baking sheet.
2.  Sprinkle with brown sugar and/or paprika, if desired.
3.  Place the bacon in a cold oven. Set the oven for 400 degrees.
4.  Bake for 18 - 30 minutes, or until the bacon reaches desired levels of
    crispiness.
