## Ingredients {#ingredients}

-   1 rotisseries chicken, shredded (about 3-4 cups)
-   1/2 cup buffalo sauce
-   1/2 cup ranch dressing
-   1 tsp Cajun seasoning
-   1/4 tsp salt
-   1/4 tsp pepper
-   2 packages King's Hawaiian rolls
-   8 oz shredded Monterey jack cheese
-   3 tbsp unsalted butter, melted
-   1/2 tsp Italian seasoning


## Directions {#directions}

1.  Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Spray a large baking dish or a rimmed baking
    sheet with cooking spray, then set aside.
2.  Place the shredded chicken in a bowl. Add buffalo sauce, ranch dressing,
    Cajun seasoning, salt and pepper. Mix to combine.
3.  Do not pull the rolls apart, simply use a large bread knife to slice the
    rolls in half as you would slice a bagel, separating them into a sheet of
    tops and bottoms. Place the layer of bottoms on the prepared baking pan, cut
    side up.
4.  Sprinkle half of the cheese evenly across the rolls and top evenly with the
    chicken mixture. Top with remaining cheese. Place the tops of the rolls on
    top.
5.  In a small bowl, stir together the melted butter and Italian seasoning. Brush
    over the tops of the sliders.
6.  Bake for 10-15 minutes or until the cheese is melted and the tops are
    slightly browned.
